# Hidden Gem Locations in Northern California

## Breweries
1. **Monk's Cellar**  
   Located in Roseville, CA, this brewery offers a cozy atmosphere and a rotating selection of craft beers made on-site.

2. **Mendocino Brewing Company**  
   Nestled in Hopland, it's known for its rich history and vibrant flavors, producing a range of organic beers.

3. **Lagunitas Brewing Company**  
   In Petaluma, this well-known brewery has a large outdoor beer garden and offers brewery tours.

## Rivers
1. **Russian River**  
   Perfect for tubing in the summer, this river flows through scenic redwood forests and offers various fishing spots.

2. **American River**  
   Known for its gold rush history, it's popular for white-water rafting and lazy summer float trips.

3. **Klamath River**  
   This river is famed for its salmon runs and beautiful landscapes, ideal for fishing and kayaking.

## Scenic Spots
1. **Point Reyes National Seashore**  
   This stunning coastline provides breathtaking views, hiking trails, and wildlife sighting opportunities.

2. **Mount Tamalpais**  
   Offers panoramic views of the Bay Area, it's a favorite spot for hikers and photographers.

3. **Lassen Volcanic National Park**  
   Known for its geothermal features, this park showcases stunning landscapes and unique hiking opportunities.
