# NorCal Adventure Story Outline

## Main Plot Structure
1. **Introduction**
   - Introduce the group of friends (Dave, Sarah, Tom, and Lisa) who decide to take a road trip to Northern California.
   - Set the comedic tone with their quirky personalities and hilarious interactions.

2. **Trip Begins**
   - A mishap with the car that leads to a funny detour.
   - Stop at a quirky roadside attraction (e.g., the World’s BiggestBeer Can).

3. **At the River**
   - Arrive at the riverbank where they plan to set up camp and enjoy beers.
   - Decision Branch:
     - **Option A**: Go fishing and accidentally catch a giant (but friendly) fish that wants to join their party.
     - **Option B**: Launch into a canoe only to have it capsize, leading to a series of comedic misadventures.

4. **The Beer Tasting Competition**
   - They participate in a local beer tasting competition.
   - Decision Branch:
     - **Option A**: They sabotage the competition in a humorous manner. (e.g., switching labels)
     - **Option B**: They end up being champions due to sheer luck.

5. **The Great Escape**
   - As the competition ends, they find themselves in a sticky situation involving a bear drawn to the beer.
   - Hilarious escape plan ensues, showcasing their teamwork and wackiness.

6. **Conclusion**
   - The friends reminisce about their adventure, realizing that the real joy was their bond and shared laughter, despite the chaos.

## Decision Branches
- Each decision affects the narrative flow, leading to different comedic outcomes and interactions.
- The reader's choices can shape the friendship dynamics and the adventure's flavor!
